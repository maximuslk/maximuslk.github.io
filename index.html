About Us

Devi Trading was established in 1971 by Mr Vengadasalam, since then we have developed a reputation as a highly specialized import and export organization with niche markets in 25 countries across Europe, the Americas, and Asia. Our passion for our products and for our customers as well as the versatility of our international marketing activities has helped us become one of the leading trading companies in Asia.

who we are

Our Mission to deliver high quality products and build productive, long-lasting relationships with our customers. We will work as a group to achieve our target of capturing the global market. To build mutual success by contributing to the success of our customers. Our success lies in our passion for quality, precision and customer satisfaction.

Our Vision we believe in making a difference. Our philosophy is one of integration and empowerment within the community. By enriching the lives of the communities we work with, we are not only fostering a better tomorrow but also have the satisfaction of knowing that our presence is benefiting the communities that we work with as well as creating quality consciousness and enhancing the capabilities of our farmers. We strive to build strong supplier and customer relationships. We are a fair trade company.

Services

Imports - The portfolio of imports is evenly balanced between industrial products and agro food items. Our imports cover a wide range of industrial chemicals, all kinds of dyestuffs and solvents. Agro food items and components are supplied all over the region with a strong supply chain around the world and local farmers.

Dyes

Dyes

We supply a range of dyes for garment manufacturers as well as companies engaging in textile art, such as batik printing, and for manufacturers of natural colour dyed products. At Devi Trading we endeavor to be a manufacturing partner rather than just a supplier. We use our years of experience to help our customers efficiently utilize the dyes they purchase. Our Quality Assurance Laboratory conducts QA checks on all our dyes to ensure that the client’s shade quality requirements are met. We are the sole agents for Sinochem Tianjin Co., China, pioneers in the field of dyestuffs and pigments, manufacturers of the best-selling “Youhao” dye and TP pigment.Details

Textiles

Textiles

Batik Manufacturing
Sri Lankans have been producing batik for over 400 years, since the time of the Portuguese. Currently, Kandy, Kegalle, Yakkala, Kadawatha, Imbulgoda, Nittambuwa, and Beruwala make up the main centers of the batik industry of Sri Lanka. Due to the increased demand by tourists for batik products, the industry is now undergoing a boom. We are proud to be a part of this process by supplying a range of customized dyes that are needed for the printing of batik materials.Details

Chemicals

Chemicals

Industrial chemicals have been a fundamental component of the Devi Trading’s International division since 1971, importing a range of inorganic and organic chemicals. The chemicals we import are used in textile, rubber, detergent manufacturing, as well as in the food and beverage industry. We work with some of the most prestigious manufacturing companies in Sri Lanka and we are proud to be a part of their success. Our Industrial Chemicals Division boasts an extensive, top-of-the-range inventory management system which ensures availability of stocks as well as our ability to meet all customer requirements. We carry out all quality assurance tests at our own R&D laboratory which was established in 2007 so that we can strictly monitor all our processes to ensure we meet all national and international standards.Details

Solvents

Solvents

Key partner for your solvent supply source, we import a wide range of solvents for use in the manufacture of paints, printing inks and packaging. Our products are in high demand in the local market because of their compositional accuracy, precise pH range, optimum effectiveness and high purity levels. Keeping the convenience of our customers in mind, we offer these chemicals in bulk quantities in tamper-proof packaging. Our efficient management system along with our policy of emphasizing service and quality since 1971 has won us the trust and confidence of local manufacturers. Our products are supplied from renowned manufacturers in China, India, Japan, Europe and the United States.Details
